Meet Samuel

Samuel overcame his learning difficulties, mental barriers and emotional strains to reach his full potential. Samuel is an influencer, an endurance athlete, and an entrepreneur!

Samuel The Influencer

Life isn’t always clear. Somedays, we all lack the motivation to get out of bed to do daily activities. We wonder how to find the motivation that can last, the motivation that can make our vision and goals reality.

Samuel was in that rut, he went through the valley of doubt and that when he started his journey to find his meaning of motivation, to define his meaning of success, and to find his peak performance.

At the age of 5 years old, he got diagnosed with dyslexia and is still battling multiple learning disabilities daily. At the age of 15, he started dealing with depression, anxiety, and suicidal because of the divorce in his family.

Pain is just the old you leaving the body. Commit to what you started and go through it.

Samuel The Endurance Athlete

After walking away from motocross, his life passion, Samuel had to redefine his aspiration. That’s when he found triathlon and endurance running where he keeps on discovering new boundaries and achieving what he once thought impossible. Samuel’s urge for speed never left. He is still riding dirt bikes, sport bikes, and moves through life as quick.

Fear holds us back; Fear is the unknown; Fear is created by our mind. Our biggest enemy is not fear, it’s our-self!

Samuel The Entrepreneur

Samuel has experience ranging from 5 to 18 years in multiple different fields. He is an owner of two businesses where he is currently building a team in the financial industry and his Coaching&Speaking business.

Samuel’s goals are beyond most people’s understanding and he believes your goals are too. Currently Samuel is working full time, operating two businesses, and continuing to seek his 100%.
